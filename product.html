Why Two Products?

Form-A: Why buy code I could write myself?

You're not buying code — you're buying time. 6 months of architecture decisions, deployment patterns, and edge-case handling compressed into a one-click deploy. The value is in the industrialization: auth system, HA failover, distributor tracking, monitoring — not the individual lines.

Form-B: Why buy a paper when code is free?

The community edition is free code. Form-B is the design thinking behind it. You buy it when you want to understand the first-principles decisions, then re-implement in your own stack using o1-preview or Claude Opus. The PDF doesn't give you fish — it teaches you how to fish, then gives you a fishing blueprint.

What's the distributor model?

Each Enterprise license embeds a distributor ID. The auth server tracks which distributor referred the customer at purchase time. Distributor commission is paid out monthly at 30% of first order + 15% renewal — fully automated, no manual tracking.

Can I switch from community to enterprise?

Yes. Deploy the community edition first (free, fully functional). When you need K8s orchestration, auth dashboard, or SLA support, upgrade to enterprise — the data and workflows migrate seamlessly.
